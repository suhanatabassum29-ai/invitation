Festivals of India

Celebrating colors, culture, and traditions

Bathukamma

A floral festival celebrated predominantly in Telangana by women with colorful flower stacks.

Holi

The festival of colors, symbolizing the victory of good over evil and the arrival of spring.

Dasara (Dussehra)

Celebrates the triumph of Lord Rama over Ravana, and good over evil.

Sankranti

A harvest festival celebrated with kites, sweets, and cultural traditions, especially in South India.

Diwali

The festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and knowledge over ignorance.

Rakhi (Raksha Bandhan)

A festival celebrating the bond between brothers and sisters, marked by tying a rakhi thread.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Celebrates the birth of Lord Ganesha, with grand processions, worship, and immersion rituals.
